HOW TO ENROLL?

Hogwarts has no admission requirements. Students interested in attending Hogwarts must complete Common Application during enrollment rounds. This will require families to create a family profile, rank Hogwarts as a preference, submit an application and wait for placement results. Students who are placed at Hogwarts will receive a notification to register by submitting the required documents to the registrar.

Families must complete Common Application to enroll at Sci High. It is recommended to rank our school as the #1 school on the application to increase chances for placement at our school.

Assistance Available - Hogwarts operates three Family Resource Centers (FRC) to assist families enrolling in Hogwarts schools and early childhood programs. Our centers are conveniently located across the city and open for walk-in support Monday-Friday from 8:30am to 3:00 pm. Family support is available via email at enroll@hogwartsschools.com or families can leave a message at +248-934-6913.
