About us

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a Scottish wizarding school located in the Scottish Highlands. It accepts any magical student residing in the islands of Great Britain and Ireland at the age of eleven years old at the start of the incoming term for enrolment. It is a state-owned school, funded by the British Ministry of Magic.learn more..

Our Facilities

The Library

The Hogwarts Library contains tens of thousands of books on thousands of shelves. Overseen by Madam Irma Pince, the library is where students could go to peruse or borrow books to supplement their studies (or for personal enjoyment).

The Cafeteria

The Great Hall is the main place where students received their meals. They also held certain special events, such as the Sorting Ceremony, and the Hallowe'en Feast. The large hall had enough space to hold every student, as well as the main staff and guests.

Sports

We provide a large playground fit for everyone to play in with various play areas and courts.We also provide all the items as well.
